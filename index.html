⏳ I just registered / deregistered and nothing changed

This is normal. The Midnight indexer can lag behind the Cardano blockchain by a few minutes to several hours. Your transaction is already on-chain — the Inspector just needs to catch up.

After deregistration, the Inspector may still show "Registered" for a while. This is the indexer lag — not a problem.

Wait 15–60 minutes, then click Refresh. If it still hasn't updated after a few hours, check GitHub Issues for any known outages.

⚠️ I see a warning or error

Duplicate / multiple registrations detected

⚠ Multiple registrations detected for this stake address.

This happens when NIGHT arrives in an already-registered wallet, causing the protocol to create a second registration automatically.

  1. 1Connect your wallet and click Deregister.
  2. 2Wait for confirmation (a few minutes).
  3. 3Click Register now once the state shows "Not registered".

Indexer unavailable

The Midnight indexer could not be reached. Try again in a few minutes.

This is temporary and does not affect your registration or NIGHT. Wait a few minutes and click Refresh.

🔒 I have locked NIGHT (Airdrop Vesting)

The Airdrop Vesting tile appears when part of your NIGHT comes from a vesting contract. These tokens are locked at a separate script address until each scheduled "thaw" date.

Countdown running: Your NIGHT unlocks automatically when the timer reaches zero. No action needed until then.

Claimable (shows "Now"): Click Claim NIGHT now → to go to redeem.midnight.gd and complete the claim.

After claiming: Check the Inspector again. Receiving new NIGHT into a registered wallet can sometimes trigger a duplicate registration — de-register and re-register if that happens.

The locked NIGHT does not count toward your DUST cap while it is in the vesting contract. It will be included once claimed.

Night Balance

The total NIGHT in your regular wallet addresses. This is what the protocol uses to calculate your DUST generation rate and cap. Higher NIGHT = more DUST per day.

Active Registration

Shows whether your NIGHT is registered for DUST generation. "Registered ✓" means DUST is accumulating in your Midnight wallet right now.

Airdrop Vesting (only visible if you have locked NIGHT)

NIGHT from the airdrop that is still locked in a vesting contract. It does not count toward your DUST cap until you claim it.

Common questions

Is this tool safe? Can it access my funds? ▼

No. The Inspector only reads public blockchain data. It never requests your seed phrase or private keys. The source code is fully open on GitHub.

Can I check someone else's address? ▼

Yes — all data is public. Enter any stake1… address. You can view but not change anything without connecting the actual wallet.

The Inspector shows different data than my Midnight wallet. Why? ▼

The Midnight indexer can lag behind the Cardano blockchain by minutes to hours. The Inspector shows both the indexer view and the actual on-chain state so you can spot discrepancies.
