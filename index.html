Midnight DUST Inspector — Help Guide

This guide explains every state the Inspector can show, what it means, and what action — if any — you should take. The Inspector is read-only and non-custodial. It never has access to your seed phrase or private keys.

What it means: Your deregistration transaction has been confirmed on the Cardano blockchain, but the Midnight indexer has not processed it yet. This is normal — the indexer can lag by minutes to hours.

Duplicate / Multiple Registrations

Action needed
⚠ Multiple registrations detected for this stake address.

What it means: The Midnight indexer has found more than one active DUST registration linked to your stake key. This can happen when NIGHT is received into an already-registered wallet, causing the protocol to create a second registration automatically.

While this does not immediately break DUST generation, it is an unexpected state that should be resolved.

What to do

  1. 1Connect your Cardano wallet in the Inspector.
  2. 2Click Deregister to remove the current registration.
  3. 3Wait for the deregistration to be confirmed on-chain (a few minutes).
  4. 4Register again once the state shows "Not registered".

The Inspector's timeline section shows a full history of all your registrations and deregistrations, which can help understand when and how duplicates occurred.

  2. 2If the error persists, check the GitHub Issues page to see if there is a known outage.
  3. 3You can still view your on-chain registration history in the Timeline section, which uses Koios (independent of the Midnight indexer).

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this tool safe to use? ▼

Yes. The Inspector only reads public blockchain data. It never asks for your seed phrase or private keys. All Cardano lookups go through the public Koios API. The source code is open and auditable on GitHub.

Why does the Inspector show different data than my Midnight wallet? ▼

The Midnight indexer can lag behind the Cardano blockchain by minutes to hours. The Inspector shows you both: the indexer's current view AND the actual on-chain state from Koios, so you can spot any discrepancy.

My locked NIGHT in the Vesting tile does not count toward DUST — is that correct? ▼

Yes. Locked vesting NIGHT sits at a script address and is excluded from the DUST cap calculation. Only NIGHT in your regular wallet addresses counts. Once you claim the vesting NIGHT and it arrives in your wallet, it will be included.

I sent NIGHT to my wallet and now I see a duplicate registration. What happened? ▼

This is a known behaviour: receiving NIGHT into an already-registered wallet can trigger an automatic re-registration by the protocol, resulting in two entries. De-register once and re-register to resolve it. See the Duplicate Registration section above.

How often does the DUST balance update? ▼

DUST accumulates continuously based on your generation rate. The balance visible in your Midnight wallet updates when the Midnight indexer processes the next block. The Inspector shows a snapshot at the time of the last check — use the Refresh button to get the latest data.

Can I check someone else's stake address? ▼

Yes. All data shown is public. Enter any stake1… address in the input field. You will see their NIGHT balance and registration state — but you will not be able to register or deregister on their behalf.
